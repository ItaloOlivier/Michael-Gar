About Michael

Michael Gar is a rising star in the world of elite triathlon, representing Great Britain on the international stage.

Born in England to South African parents, with Italian heritage on his mother's side, Michael brings a unique international perspective to the sport. At just 21 years old, he's already making waves in elite-level competition.

Currently balancing his Finance and Accounting degree at Cardiff University with elite-level training, Michael exemplifies dedication and passion. He's part of the Welsh Triathlon National Talent Performance Centre (NTPCW) and has been racing with the prestigious 707 Team Minini in Italy for over a year.

"It's challenging balancing university and elite-level sport, but it's the lifestyle I love. My objective is becoming a professional triathlete after graduation."
